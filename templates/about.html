About FloraScan

FloraScan is an innovative AI-powered plant identification platform dedicated to unlocking the secrets of Philippine flora. Our mission is to make plant identification accessible, accurate, and educational for everyone.

Our Mission

We believe that understanding and appreciating the rich biodiversity of the Philippines starts with proper identification. FloraScan leverages cutting-edge artificial intelligence to help users identify plants quickly and accurately, fostering a deeper connection with nature.

How It Works

Simply upload a clear photo of any plant, and our advanced AI system analyzes its features, leaves, flowers, and other characteristics to provide you with accurate identification results. Whether you're a botanist, gardener, or nature enthusiast, FloraScan makes plant identification simple and accessible.

Philippine Flora

The Philippines is home to thousands of unique plant species, many of which are endemic to the archipelago. FloraScan specializes in identifying these diverse species, helping preserve and document the incredible botanical heritage of the Philippines.
